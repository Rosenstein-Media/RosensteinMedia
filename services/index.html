Services

+ Personal

Personal sessions are intended for individual use. These are relaxed, studio-forward sessions designed to be accessible while still intentional.

$50 — Mini Studio Session

~30 minutes, on-site studio session. One look with a single lighting setup and a few different poses. Ideal for LinkedIn-style headshots or quick portraits.

Includes approximately 5 clean, edited images delivered electronically.

$125 — Full Studio Session

~1 hour, on-site studio session with multiple looks and lighting setups. Allows time for outfit changes and added flexibility in the shoot.

Includes approximately 15 final edited images delivered electronically.

+ $50 — Analog Upgrade

Upgrade either studio session with a roll of film. Choice of black & white or color, 35mm or medium format.

Film is scanned and delivered electronically alongside your digital images.

+100 — +1HR

Each additional hour of studio time is charged at $100 per hour.

$350+ — Off-Site / On-Location (Personal)

Personal sessions outside the studio start at $250 and account for travel, setup, and location variables. Pricing may vary depending on scope.

If you ever just want to kick it and shoot skate photos, that’s free.

+ Commercial

Commercial photography is intended to promote a brand, product, service, or individual in a paid or revenue-generating context.

This includes (but is not limited to): brand campaigns, advertisements, sponsored skaters, album artwork, apparel, websites, and social media used for promotion.

Commercial Rates

Commercial projects are priced separately based on usage, scope, deliverables, and duration. Rates typically start higher than personal sessions.

Please reach out with details about the project and intended usage for a custom quote.

+ Lab

I offer bespoke black & white film development services, as well as color and black & white scanning services.

I offer these services because my local lab's turn-around time for black & white is 6-8 weeks. If you need your black & white film processed faster than that, I can do it for you. Your film gets processed within my workflow.

My workflow varies; accordingly turn-around time may vary, but is typically no longer than two weeks.

B&W Development

35mm: $5 per roll, minimum 2 rolls.

Medium Format: $10 per roll. No minimum.

Scanning Services

35mm: $5 per roll.

Medium Format: $5 per roll.

Negative Conversion

Negative conversion is included in scanning services. Please note, I cannot spend time perfecting your scans. These are basic scans intended for digital use. I can however return both negative scans and a raw digital positive file for your own editing.

+ Graphics

My work goes beyond capturing images: Each photo becomes a building block for compelling visual content. I design ads, graphics, logos, posters, and other marketing materials, using photography strategically to communicate a brand’s message. Please contact me for a quote.
