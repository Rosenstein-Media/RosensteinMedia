A Personal Note

Hello:

Thank you for visiting my website.

I know it is not the most flashy and I must apologize if it looks or feels clunky. While I appreciate the offers I have received to improve this site, I need to be able to add, tweak, and adapt content as time goes on. To do that, I need to retain complete control while working with my limited knowledge of web design and development. To that end, I built this site entirely myself starting with zero knowledge of HTML, CSS, or Javascript. I designed all logos, and you guessed it, I created all the content that fills the pages too. I also did it this way to minimize cost: I only pay a yearly fee for the domain name and not even for hosting (i.e., server space).

It took a lot of work and different skills to get here and I am quite proud of it.

And so again, thank you.
